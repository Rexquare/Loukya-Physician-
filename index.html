Our Story

The Clinic That
Restores Lives

Loukya Pain Management Clinic was founded with a singular vision — to bring world-class physiotherapy and rehabilitation care to the heart of Hyderabad.

Our Mission

We believe pain should never define a person's life. Our mission is to deliver evidence-based, compassionate physiotherapy that addresses the root cause of pain — not just the symptoms. Every patient deserves to live fully and freely.

Modern Approach

We combine advanced diagnostic techniques with the latest physiotherapy modalities — from manual therapy and electrotherapy to neurological rehabilitation. Our approach is scientific, holistic, and deeply personalized for each patient.

Our Values

Integrity. Empathy. Excellence. These three pillars guide every interaction at Loukya Clinic. We treat every patient as family — with dignity, patience, and genuine care for their recovery journey.

The Team

Led by Dr. Loukya, our team of dedicated physiotherapy professionals brings deep expertise across neurological, orthopedic, paediatric, and sports rehabilitation — creating a multidisciplinary care ecosystem.

Our Specialties

Comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation services tailored to your unique needs — from acute pain to chronic conditions.

Neurological Physiotherapy
Specialized rehabilitation for neurological conditions including stroke, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and brain injury. Our evidence-based approach restores movement, balance, and coordination.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Gentle, play-based physiotherapy for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, torticollis, and other paediatric conditions. We help children reach their full physical potential.
